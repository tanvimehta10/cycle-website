Motorized Bike

We have a great selection of beautiful and high quality motorized bicycles that feels just like riding a bike chopper mini. These gas powered bicycles come assembly at not extra cost (like the whizzer). This is a innovative option for those that are looking for something more powerful and faster than a electric bike or a gas scooter. Feel the wind in your face as you reach speeds of over 40 M.P.H. You have the choice to pedal like a normal bike or ride it like a motorcycle, bike chopper mini or a vespa. Another advantage of having a gas powered bicycle is that they are easier to maintain than an electric bike , whizzer,gas scooter, motorcycle,electric scooter or motorbike; and is more affordable than a chopper bike or a motorcycle. The bikes comes ready to ride with the motor installed.
